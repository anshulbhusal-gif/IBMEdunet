FUNDING OPTIONS & INVESTOR DIRECTORY (Reference for Startup Blueprint Generator)

STAGES OF STARTUP FUNDING

1. BOOTSTRAPPING (Pre-Seed / Idea Stage)
- Self-funding, savings, or funds from family and friends
- Typical amount: Under INR 10 lakh
- Best for: Validating idea, building MVP, testing initial demand

2. SEED STAGE
- First formal external funding round
- Sources: Angel investors, seed-stage VCs, government seed fund schemes (e.g. SISFS)
- Typical amount: INR 20 lakh to INR 2 crore
- Used for: Product development, initial team hiring, early market entry

3. SERIES A
- Sources: Venture Capital firms
- Typical amount: INR 4 crore to INR 40 crore
- Used for: Scaling operations, expanding team, entering new markets, proven product-market fit required

4. SERIES B AND BEYOND
- Larger VC rounds and growth equity funds
- Used for: Major scaling, geographic expansion, acquisitions

FUNDING SOURCE CATEGORIES

Angel Investors and Angel Networks (India)
- Indian Angel Network (IAN)
- Mumbai Angels
- Lead Angels
- Chennai Angels
- AngelList India

Venture Capital Firms (India-focused, early-stage)
- Sequoia Capital India (Peak XV Partners)
- Accel India
- Blume Ventures
- Kalaari Capital
- Matrix Partners India
- 3one4 Capital
- Nexus Venture Partners

Incubators and Accelerators
- T-Hub (Hyderabad)
- NASSCOM 10,000 Startups
- Startup India Seed Fund network incubators
- IIT/IIM incubation cells (e.g. SINE-IIT Bombay, CIIE-IIM Ahmedabad)
- Venture Center (Pune) - relevant for Pune-based startups, focused on science and technology startups

Crowdfunding Platforms
- Ketto, Wishberry (reward/donation-based)
- Equity crowdfunding is restricted in India under current SEBI regulations

Government-backed Funding
- Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)
- SIDBI Fund of Funds
- Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS)

MATCHING GUIDANCE FOR AGENT
- Idea stage / no product yet -> suggest bootstrapping, friends/family, government seed grants, incubator applications
- MVP built, early users -> suggest angel investors, angel networks, seed-stage VCs, SISFS
- Proven traction, revenue -> suggest Series A VCs matching the sector
- Always mention that actual investor outreach requires warm introductions, a pitch deck, and financial projections; this list is a starting point for research, not a guarantee of funding
